The OATH

_________________________

In taking the OATH, I promise that:

  • I will spread the word about adolescent obesity
  • I will respect and accept everyone in my community
  • I will be proactive about my own health


    • By taking the OATH, I show a commitment to recognizing that adolescent obesity is a serious issue in our society.

    Things You Can Do

    Things to think about when taking the OATH

    _________________________

    If you are a/an...



    Adolescent or Parent

  • If you are a parent, have conversations with your child at least a few times a year
  • Implement better eating and exercise habits and find healthier substitutes for processed foods
  • Make goals as a family (e.g. "As a family we will eat less processed foods")


    • Health Care Provider

  • Flag the system for any adolescents who have shown a steady weight gain/above average BMI​​
  • Provide brochures in waiting rooms
  • Host monthly support group meetings for adolescents with obesity​
  • Use bigger chairs in common rooms that accomodate patients who have obesity​


    • Organization

  • Take the OATH to host two health fairs that include family members of their employees ​​
  • Allow days off for employees to take their children to a doctor for obesity​​
  • Include healthier options in cafeterias​​
