Why OATH?

We aim to change the way in which the world views adolescent obesity

20.6%

of adolescents ages 12-19 have obesity 1



75-80%

of adolescents with obesity will have obesity in adulthood 2





Obesity rates by state 9

Adolescents with obesity are bullied by...

Classmates

The primary reason adolescents are teased or bullied at school is because of their weight3



Parents

37% of adolescents reported weight based victimization came from parents4



HCPs

If a doctor spoke about a child's weight in a stigmatizing way,

  • 35% of parents would switch doctors
  • 24% would avoid future medical appointments5
